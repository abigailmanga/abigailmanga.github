Abigail Manga

Dreamer. Leader. Change-Maker.

My name is Abigail Manga, and I am a third-year Information Systems major at the University of Maryland College Park. I chose information systems to expand my knowledge of how to leverage technology to solve complex problems. I am currently in the QUEST Honors Program at UMD, which focuses on problem-solving and completing experiential coursework within teams. Furthermore, I serve as the President of UMD's chapter of the National Association of Black Accountants, where I am responsible for the overall operations of the organization and play a key role in building community within the business school and helping students develop professionally.
